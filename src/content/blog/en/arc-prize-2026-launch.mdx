## ARC Prize 2026 is Live

Today we're officially announcing the start of **ARC Prize 2026**! This is the largest ARC Prize competition to date, offering over **$2,000,000** in prizes across three tracks.

### Three Tracks

1. **ARC-AGI-3 Competition Track** — Interactive reasoning benchmark, prizes for top-performing AI agents
2. **ARC-AGI-2 Competition Track** — Static reasoning benchmark, prizes for top scores on ARC-AGI-2
3. **Paper Prize Track** — Awards for papers that advance our understanding of how to achieve strong performance on ARC-AGI

### Key Dates

- **March 25, 2026** — Competition starts
- **June 30, 2026** — ARC-AGI-3 Milestone #1
- **September 30, 2026** — ARC-AGI-3 Milestone #2
- **November 2, 2026** — Submissions due
- **November 8, 2026** — Papers due
- **December 4, 2026** — Results announced

### Open Source Spirit

All leading participants are expected to open source their solutions to be eligible for a prize. The primary mission of ARC Prize is to accelerate progress toward open AGI by making cutting-edge solutions freely available to the entire research community.

We're partnering with Kaggle to ensure fair evaluation and global visibility. Join us in pushing the boundaries of AGI!
